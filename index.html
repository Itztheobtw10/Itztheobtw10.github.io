Projects / Courses

Mini Robot Coffee Barista

As a young developer, I designed a miniature robotic barista that engages customers in a friendly and personalized manner. Upon customer entry, the robot initiates a conversation by asking how they are, followed by requesting their name to be placed on their coffee cup. The barista then displays the menu on its interface, allowing the customer to make their order selection. To streamline the ordering process, I created a numbered menu with corresponding prices. Once the customer has finalized their order, the barista calculates the total order cost and presents the customer with a tipping option.
